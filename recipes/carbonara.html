Carbonara

Truly authentic Italian carbonara with bacon recipe.

Ingredients

  • 200g of fettucinni pasta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g of parmesan cheese
  • 50g of bacon
  • salt at tasty

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until the bacon be fryed
  2. Mix the eggs, parmesan and salt
  3. When the bacon is done, put the pasta in the same fridge and mix everything
